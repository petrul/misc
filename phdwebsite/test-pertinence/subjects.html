Grand prix

The grand prix of this test is of course a marvellous USB key, accorded to one of those whose choices correspond most with what finally turns out to be the most interesting answer. The test's introduction specified this in order to simulate "natural" conditions of conversation, in which people do their best at being relevant i.e. saying things that supposedly will be found relevant by the audience.

The score was established as follows: for each story, if a user chose the option that finally turned up to be the winner, he/she has got one point. Otherwise, no point. The maximum score is 20 (as there were 20 stories) the minimum is zero but fortunately nobody got it though some really tried. Only users having completed the test were taken into account.
